## üìä PATTERN SUMMARY

### ‚úÖ **ALL GOOD CONTENT HAS:**
1. **Specific data** (numbers, percentages, study size)
2. **Mechanism** (how/why it works)
3. **Actionable insight** (what to do with this info)
4. **Completeness** (if question posed, it's answered)
5. **Evidence** (citations, studies, research)
6. **Human voice** (not corporate, not buzzwords)

### ‚ùå **ALL BAD CONTENT HAS:**
1. **Buzzwords** (BREAKING, REVOLUTIONARY, POWER)
2. **Open questions** (no answers provided)
3. **Vague claims** ("research shows", "studies found")
4. **Promotional tone** (JOIN TODAY, DISCOVER THE POWER)
5. **No data** (no numbers, no citations)
6. **No substance** (meta-commentary, not teaching)
